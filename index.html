About Me

I am a passionate software developer specializing in Unity C# development. As a Lead Programmer at AlterEyes, I have extensive experience managing small developer teams and collaborating across departments to deliver high-quality games and B2B applications.

With a proven track record of shipping titles across PS5 VR, SteamVR, Meta Quest, and Pico, I excel at building core gameplay systems, implementing multiplayer features, and creating efficient editor tools to streamline production.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Independent Game Production from Howest (DAE).
